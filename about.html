Genesis of ANJUMAN ERAQUEE INDIA

Some people think they are in the community, but they are only in proximity. True community requires commitment and openness. It is a willingness to extend yourself to encounter and know the other. It is not more bigness that should be our goal. We must attempt, rather, to bring people back to the warmth of community, to the worth of individual effort and responsibility, and of individuals working together as a community, to better their lives and their children’s future.

GENESIS

The genesis of Anjuman Eraquee(Eraquee Foundation): It is but natural to be aware of the good and evil of the social unit in which we are born. Poverty, illiteracy, malnutrition, dowry, diseases, and unemployment have been widespread in our society. Regular erosion of employment and business opportunities have become normal. There are many rich and privileged people in our society but their contribution to solving social and political problems is not at par. At the same time, due to some people getting involved in the liquor business, the entire Sheikh Eraquee, Kalal have been following the practice of looking down on society from time to time due to which the mental development of youth has been blocked. Today, this society has no part in the liquor business. Some historians gave evidence of the word Kalal to be a liquor business, which established a false belief. "Firoz ul lugaat" a book in Urdu dictionary, tried to establish the tradition of discrimination to the full that Kalal means a liquor businessman. Let it be known that the name Kalal has been sacred since its origin. The names of Arab Chieftains and Emperors were also known as Abd-Kalal. Even in the Old Testament of the Holy Bible, the name of Bakht's son is also written as Kalal. Kalal also means crowned and businessman but the dishonesty of historians made it less and Kalali was awarded liquor and Kalal was conferred with liquor businessman, which can be called a discriminatory action by historians. The reason which forced the Sheikh Eraquee, Kalal, to realize their real status and make them the mainstay of development. As a result, efforts being made for decades could not meet their objectives. In such circumstances, how long a social and political worker like me could remain silent. Hence, I started a Public awakening by forming a WhatsApp group called Anjuman Iraqi now, Anjuman Eraquee, which got good returns. Meetings started being held. Working teams are now ready. If everything goes well, tomorrow will be ours.”
